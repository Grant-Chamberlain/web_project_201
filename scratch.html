ABOUT THIS PLAYLIST

What makes a song all-time worthy? Here's how I think about it:

  1. It has to hold up after 100 listens
    • The melody is still interesting years later
    • The production doesn't feel dated
  2. It has to hit emotionally
    • Lyrics that mean something
    • A bridge or moment that gives you chills
    • Bonus points for a great guitar or piano riff
  3. It has to be great for any mood
    • Driving at night ✓
    • Studying ✓
    • Just sitting and thinking ✓

All six songs on this list pass every test.
