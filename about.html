About Me

My Story

I'm Grant Chamberlain, a Computer Science student at Brigham Young University originally from Las Vegas, Nevada. Growing up I was passionate about music — I've played piano for over 11 years and string bass for 7, even earning a spot in the Clark County School District Honor Orchestra.

From 2022 to 2024 I served as a full-time missionary in McAllen, Texas, where I became proficient in Spanish and had the chance to serve a vibrant community. That experience deepened my work ethic and love for connecting with people from different backgrounds.

I'm now back at BYU pursuing software engineering, and I'm excited about where technology and service can intersect.
